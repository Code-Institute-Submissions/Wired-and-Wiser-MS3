What are smart meters?

Smart meters record your consumption data and automatically send this information over wireless networks to your supplier.

Why get a smart meter?

Estimated meter reads will become a thing of the past with your new smart meter. Smart meters send your consumption data directly to your supplier meaning you will always be billed accurately based on what you have actually used. Your in home display will help you monitor your usage in real-time meaning you can identify energy saving opportunities. By reducing your energy usage you will be reducing your carbon footprint and the impact on your wallet!
