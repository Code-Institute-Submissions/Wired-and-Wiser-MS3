What are smart meters?

Smart meters replace your existing gas and electricity meters. They use wireless networks, similar to mobile phone networks, to send data directly to your energy supplier about how much gas and electricity you're using. This means you won’t have to read your gas and electricity meters any more, and your bills won’t be estimated.

Smart meters are different from energy monitors. While energy monitors can show you how much electricity you’re using, they don’t communicate this information to your provider. When Wired & Wiser install your smart meter, we will provide you with an in home display which will show you your energy usage in real-time.

To find out more, read the Which? guide to smart meters.

Why get a smart meter?

Estimated meter reads will become a thing of the past with your new smart meter. Smart meters send your consumption data directly to your supplier meaning you will always be billed accurately based on what you have actually used. This will remove the costs of manual meter readings which are currently added to your bills.

Since Wired & Wiser are installing the latest generation of smart meters, you can switch supplier safe in the knowledge that your new supplier will continue to receive your consumption data.

Furthermore, your in home display will help you monitor your usage in real-time meaning you can identify energy saving opportunities in your home or business. By changing your everyday habits, you can reduce your energy usage, your carbon footprint and the impact on your wallet!

Checkout the Energy Saving Trust for energy saving tips.
